Friends,

We are thrilled to invite you to our wedding on June 7, 2025!

As you know, we have dedicated our lives to making the world a better place. We want our wedding to reflect that purpose—an aim beyond ourselves.

Weddings, at their heart, are about community: sharing our love with the people we love. They are also public commitments, asking those around us to help hold us accountable as we grow together.

To prepare, we’ve challenged ourselves, including learning how to dance! We want to extend a challenge to you, too: instead of spending the day talking with those you already know, we encourage you to engage with the incredible community we’ve built over the years.

With that in mind, our wedding will feature some non-traditional activities to push you out of your comfort zone and foster new connections. When Sean was young, he went skiing with his father. After a full day on the slopes, he proudly announced he hadn’t fallen once. His father replied, “If you’re not falling, you’re not growing.” We invite you to join us in the spirit of growth and connection.
